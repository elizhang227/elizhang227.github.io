Few Words About Me

Hi there! I'm Eli. I'm from New York City born and raised but currently residing in Atlanta. I was a fresh college graduate with no plans in pursuing a career in the subject I studied. Bills needed to be paid so I worked as front desk agent at a hotel, but being there made me realize that I needed to do something mentally stimulating and challenging which has lead me to a new found love and passion for coding.

I am currently enrolled at a coding bootcamp called DigitalCrafts located at the Atlanta Tech Village. When I am not typing away in VSCode I am either playing some sort of game whether that be on console, pc or mobile, or going to the gym and wondering why I'm making no progress!

Take a look around and don't forget to connect — whether that's a tweet, email or on the battlefield!
